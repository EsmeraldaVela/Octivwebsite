The Team

Octiv is made up of many individuals who are intelligent and friendly. Most of the individuals had majored in a different course other than technology. Their pathway to Octiv have been all different. Some became interested in technology because of curiosity.

Even though she is part of the marketing team, she still works closely with the other departments. She was one of the very firsts members to begin at Octiv.

Daniel Bell

Daniel Bell is the web designer at Octiv. He is also part of the marketing team. His position compromise of updating the home page for Octiv and adding new cool features. He loves to work with CSS.

Aaron Lucas is part of the support team. His position consists of hiring new employees, online training, and keeping articles up to date on the website. Mary Ryan

Mary is the executive assistant to the CEO of Octiv. Her job compromises of helping the Chief Executive Officer and making things easier for him.
