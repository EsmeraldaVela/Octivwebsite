About Octiv

Octiv is a tech company located in Indianapolis. Octiv was formed in 2010 and was previously known as Tinderbox. It is a scale-up company that provides fast working documents. Octiv products are online and saves time by having fantastic features like auto-save. Also, the documents provide real-time collaboration. Octiv works with companies of any size and it is available in 200 countries. It is offered in more than 20 international languages. As of now, Octiv is going be acquired by Conga.
